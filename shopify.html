Introduction:

This dashboard captures the full Shopify sales journey, from transactions and payment gateways to customer retention and product trends. It provides a strategic overview of what drives revenue, where customers are located, and how they behave post-purchase.

Objectives:

  • Monitor sales growth and customer repeat behavior
  • Visualize sales trends by city and region
  • Evaluate product category performance across customer segments
  • Understand the breakdown of payment methods used during checkout

Key Insights:

  • The majority of customers who made purchases returned for repeat orders, reflecting a healthy retention rate
  • Certain cities stood out as high-performing sales zones
  • Shopify Payments was the most preferred gateway, followed by third-party options
  • Athletic footwear categories dominated in product sales
  • Periodic revenue spikes were observed, likely influenced by promotions or seasonal sales
